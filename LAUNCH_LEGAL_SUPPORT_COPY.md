## Listening notice

### Short version

```text
Tāla saves your choices, not the audio.
```

### Public product version

```text
Tāla streams archival recordings from their original source and saves only your choices and settings. It does not save, render, or export source audio.
```

### Rights-respecting version

```text
Tāla is for private listening and discovery. Please do not record, download, export, redistribute, or commercially use source archive audio or captured blends without permission from the relevant rights holders.
```

## Terms-style product notice

```text
Tāla pairs an original beat with a stream from its original source for live listening. Favorite Blends save only stream references and settings such as beat choice, tempo, swing, and volume. Tāla does not transfer ownership of archival recordings and does not grant redistribution rights.
```

## Privacy-style product notice

```text
Tāla stores the account information and Favorite Blend settings needed to reopen your saved blends. Tāla does not store source archive audio as part of Favorite Blends.
```

## Archive acknowledgement

```text
Archive acknowledgement

Many recordings in Tāla are streamed from Archive of Indian Music and remain connected to their original archive source. Tāla exists to help more people discover, revisit, and listen closely to historic recordings through playful interaction.
```
