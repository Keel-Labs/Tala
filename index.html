About

Tāla is an ephemeral listening platform for reviving historic recordings. Why? Rare music and voice recordings that carry the voices of our ancestors are disappearing at an alarming rate. Tāla was created to help people discover some of the earliest recordings of Indian music, engage with them by mixing in beats, and rekindle interest in vintage recordings. Tracks are sourced from the Archive of Indian Music.

Favorite Blends

If a blend feels right, save the stream, beat, volumes, tempo, and swing to your account. Favorite Blends save choices and settings only, never source audio. A one-time paid unlock helps cover the cost of maintaining accounts, preferences, and saved favorites for each listener.

Listening Notice

Archive recordings remain subject to the rights and terms of their original source and rights holders. Tāla streams them for private listening only and does not record, download, export, or distribute source audio. Tāla does not own these recordings or their underlying rights. Track availability depends on the Archive of Indian Music and other connected streaming sources.
