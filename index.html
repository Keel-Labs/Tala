Tāla

Built by Medha Ghatikesh at Keel Labs. Hand crafted in California for archival discovery, playful listening, and cultural memory.

Archive & Purpose

Recordings stream from Archive of Indian Music on SoundCloud. Tāla is a way to discover historic recordings by playing with rhythm beside them and hearing them with fresh ears.

Favorite Blends

If a blend feels right, save the stream, beat, volumes, tempo, and swing to your account. Favorite Blends save choices and settings, not source audio.

Listening Notice

Tāla is for private listening. Please do not record, download, export, redistribute, or commercially use archive audio or captured blends without permission.
