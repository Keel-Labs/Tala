Tāla

Privacy

Last updated June 26, 2026

Tāla is designed to keep listening ephemeral. Tāla does not record or store source audio from archival streams.

What Tāla stores

If you create an account and unlock Favorite Blends, Tāla may store your email address, the stream URL you chose, your selected beat, and your listening settings such as volume, tempo, swing, and effects.

Basic visit analytics

Tāla also stores anonymous visit events so Keel Labs can understand total visits and approximate unique visitors. These analytics do not store source audio.

What Tāla does not store

Tāla does not store the archival source audio, rendered remixes, or exported audio files.

Third-party services

Tāla relies on third-party services including Supabase for authentication and account storage, Lemon Squeezy for payments, and streaming providers such as SoundCloud and Spotify for embedded playback.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to hello@keel-labs.org.
