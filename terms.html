Tāla is an ephemeral listening platform for discovering and interacting with historic recordings. By using Tāla, you agree to use it only in ways that respect the rights, terms, and availability of the original streaming sources.

Use of archival streams

Tracks available through Tāla are streamed from third-party sources, including the Archive of Indian Music and SoundCloud. Tāla does not own these recordings or their underlying rights.

You may use Tāla for private listening. You may not use Tāla to record, download, export, redistribute, or commercially exploit source audio or captured blends unless you have permission from the relevant rights holders.

Favorite Blends

Favorite Blends is a one-time paid unlock. It saves your chosen stream URL, beat choice, and listening settings to your account. It does not save source audio.

Availability

Tāla depends on connected streaming sources and embedded players. Streams, metadata, and playback behavior may change or disappear if those sources change or remove content.
